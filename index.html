🔥 Warm-Up (5-10 minutes)

  • 5 minutes light cardio (jumping jacks, jogging in place)
  • Dynamic stretching (arm circles, leg swings, torso twists)
  • Mobility work for target muscles
  • 1-2 warm-up sets at 50% working weight

🧘 Cool-Down (5-10 minutes)

  • Static stretching for all worked muscles (30s each)
  • Deep breathing exercises
  • Foam rolling tight areas
  • Hydrate and refuel within 30 minutes

🎉 Rest Day - Recovery Mode

Great work this week! Today is for recovery. Your muscles grow during rest.

🧘 Active Recovery

  • Light stretching or yoga (20-30 min)
  • Walking or easy cycling
  • Swimming or light sports

💆 Recovery Tools

  • Foam rolling tight muscles
  • Massage or self-myofascial release
  • Ice bath or contrast therapy

🍽️ Nutrition & Sleep

  • Eat adequate protein (1.6-2.2g/kg)
  • Stay hydrated (3-4 liters water)
  • Get 7-9 hours quality sleep
